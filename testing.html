Testing Information Delivery


While systems already in place (such as SONYC, ACOEM, and Nonoise.org) are extremely advanced, they almost solely focus only on the collection of the data, leaving the rest up to the DEP.

SONYC, ACOEM, and Nonoise.org, all share the common strength of being able to accurately record real time data of noise levels. However, taking these systems into consideration, they still have a ways to go in order to fully mitigate noise regulation. For all these systems, it seems that they still look to NYC’s Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) to handle the situation past the point of information gathering. Whilst this remedies the issue to a certain extent, the turnaround time can be slow, short-lasting and overall unhelpful to citizens a majority of the time.
